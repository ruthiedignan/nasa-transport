Project Context

Before the coronavirus pandemic, there was not significant uncertainty regarding the future of the economy. Sectors including shipping, travel, and shopping now face an unprecedented future. As a result, the workplace and general mobility trends worldwide have drastically changed. It is our goal to predict answers to these five guiding questions regarding the future of the coronavirus pandemic:

  • How will switching between different commerce mediums (i.e. physical, online) impact the economy during national emergencies and after?
  • What will the future of the workplace (in terms of transport) look like after the pandemic?
  • Why have supply chains and different forms of mobility failed to cope with the demands of this pandemic?
